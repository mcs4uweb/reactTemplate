Typical Filing Package for Your Situation

  • Form 1040 (main return)
  • Schedule B (for interest/dividends)
  • Schedule A (only if itemizing deductions)
  • Form 2441 (if claiming childcare credit)
  • All supporting documents (W-2, 1099s, 1098)

‚úÖ Most common scenario:

  • Take standard deduction ($29,200 for MFJ in 2024)
  • File Form 1040 + Schedule B
  • Claim Child Tax Credit directly on Form 1040

Critical Reminders

  • Roth IRA contributions: No tax deduction, but keep records for future withdrawals
  • 401(k) contributions: Already excluded from your W-2 income (Box 1)
  • Car expenses: Only deductible if used for business (not personal)
  • State taxes: You'll also need to file state returns (forms vary by state)
